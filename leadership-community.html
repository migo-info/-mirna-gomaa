05 · Guest Speaker · Queen Mary University of London

Taking storytelling
into the world

After completing my studies at Queen Mary University of London, I returned to deliver a public speaking workshop for undergraduate students, focusing on practical communication skills applicable to academic, professional, and personal settings.

The session drew directly on my artistic practice as a live storytelling performer — using the principles of oral narrative to help students communicate with more depth, clarity, and humanity.
