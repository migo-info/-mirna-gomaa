Three years on
an international stage

"The Creative Forum brings together independent performing arts practitioners from across Egypt, Europe, and the Mediterranean region."

I was first invited to participate in the Alex Arts Center's Creative Forum for Independent Theater Groups in 2022, as a storytelling artist. Participation in the Creative Forum is by professional invitation only.

From 2022 through to 2024 I contributed live oral storytelling performances to the Creative Forum's programme of events. My practice focuses on the relationship between personal narrative and professional life — bringing the art of oral storytelling into dialogue with corporate and institutional settings.

On 15 August 2024 I performed live on stage at the Alex Arts Center as part of the Creative Forum's 2024 programme. The audience included international artists and practitioners from across the Euro-Mediterranean region.

About the Alex Arts Center

The Alex Arts Center is part of the Bibliotheca Alexandrina — one of the most significant cultural institutions in the world, built to revive the spirit of the Ancient Library of Alexandria. Its Arts Center hosts Egyptian and international contemporary arts and maintains partnerships with cultural institutions across multiple countries.

The Creative Forum · International Programme

A Euro-Mediterranean
exchange of artists

The Creative Forum for Independent Theater Groups is one of the most significant international artistic exchange programmes operating in the Arab world. It brings together independent performing arts practitioners from Egypt, Europe, and the Mediterranean region for a structured programme of live performances, intercultural dialogues, professional training, and artistic publications.

Award · Recognition · 2024

Outstanding Achievement
in Storytelling

جائزة التميز في السرد الإبداعي · Alex Arts Center · Alexandria Center of Arts

Following my performance on 15 August 2024, and in recognition of the quality and consistency of my artistic contribution to the Creative Forum programme from 2022 to 2024, I was presented with the Outstanding Achievement in Storytelling Award by the Alex Arts Center.

The award is conferred on artists who have demonstrated exceptional artistic quality and sustained commitment within the Creative Forum programme. It is not awarded routinely and reflects a formal institutional recognition of an artist's standing as a developing storytelling practitioner of international significance.

As I am based in London, UK, I was unable to collect the physical award in person at the time of the ceremony. The trophy was brought to London subsequently and I am photographed here holding it.
