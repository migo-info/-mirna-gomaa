Storytelling Artist · London, UK

"If London were a man, I would marry this man."

Mirna Gomaa

I am a storytelling and spoken word artist performing across live cultural platforms in London and internationally. My practice sits at the intersection of narrative performance, personal testimony, and audience participation — where a single story becomes a shared experience.

With a background spanning Egyptian cultural institutions and UK spoken word stages, I bring cross-cultural depth to every performance. My style is direct, intimate, and unafraid of silence — shaped by years of listening, performing, and refining the craft of live narrative.

My Mission

To bring the art of storytelling into the corporate world. The corporate world can feel grey. Storytelling makes it colourful.
