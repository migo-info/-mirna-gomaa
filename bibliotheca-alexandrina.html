Bibliotheca Alexandrina · Arts Center

I had the honour of performing as a storytelling artist at the Bibliotheca Alexandrina Arts Center in Alexandria, Egypt. The Bibliotheca Alexandrina is one of the most culturally significant institutions in the world — a revival of the Ancient Library of Alexandria and a meeting point for scientists, philosophers, and artists from across the globe.

The Arts Center promotes creative exchange and mutual understanding between cultures through building partnerships with institutions from different backgrounds. Being invited to perform there as a storytelling artist represents a meaningful recognition of my work and its reach beyond the United Kingdom.

The performance was part of the Arts Center's ongoing commitment to hosting international contemporary arts and facilitating experience sharing between local and international artists.

About the venue

The Bibliotheca Alexandrina aims to revive the role of the Ancient Library of Alexandria. Its Arts Center provides a forum dedicated to various expressions of all forms of art, supporting Egyptian and high-profile international contemporary arts from around the world.

About the Bibliotheca Alexandrina

Egypt's window
on the world

The Bibliotheca Alexandrina is one of the most remarkable cultural institutions in the world. Built to revive the spirit of the Ancient Library of Alexandria, it serves as a meeting place for scientists, philosophers, and artists from around the globe.

Its Arts Center promotes creative exchange and mutual understanding between cultures, hosting both Egyptian and international contemporary arts. It has built partnerships with institutions from different backgrounds and regularly presents Egyptian contemporary arts in venues abroad.

International Arts Program

The Arts Center hosts international artists through its Artist in Residence program and presents Egyptian contemporary arts in venues abroad, facilitating experience sharing between local and international artists.

Cultural Exchange

Monthly events support Egyptian and high-profile international contemporary arts from around the world, building partnerships with institutions from different national and cultural backgrounds.

Euro-Mediterranean Forum
